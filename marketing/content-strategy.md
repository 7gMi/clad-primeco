## 1. Brand Voice Guidelines

### Voice Attributes
- **Direct** — Pas de jargon inutile. Aller droit au but.
- **Confident** — On connaît notre métier. On le prouve par les projets.
- **Technical but Accessible** — Précis techniquement, compréhensible pour un project manager.
- **Reliable** — Tenir les promesses. "On programme, on budget."

### Tone per Channel
| Channel | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Website | Professionnel, rassurant, orienté résultats |
| Instagram | Visuel, fier du travail, coulisses authentiques |
| LinkedIn | Expert, thought leadership, industry insights |
| Email | Personnel, direct, orienté action |

### Messaging Framework
- **Tagline** : "Cladding and Roofing Bespoke Specialists"
- **Value Proposition** : One specialist team for your entire cladding package — Kingspan panels, architectural facades, aluminium roof deck — from first fixing to final sign-off.
- **Proof Points** : 20+ projects delivered, 20,000+ m² installed, Carrigtwohil College, Amazon, Bausch & Lomb
- **Differentiator** : Single-source cladding contractor — no subcontracting, no coordination headaches

### Language Rules
- Always: "we deliver", "we install", "on programme"
- Never: "cheap", "discount", "best price"
- Technical terms OK: U-values, fire rating A2-s1,d0, Part L compliance
- Always specify: project name, location, system used

## 2. Content Pillars (5)

### Pillar 1: Project Showcases (30%)
- **Purpose** : Prouver l'expertise par les réalisations
- **Target** : Main contractors, developers
- **Formats** : Case studies, before/after, photo galleries, time-lapses
- **Frequency** : 2x/semaine
- **Examples** : Carrigtwohil College case study, Amazon warehouse gallery

### Pillar 2: Technical Knowledge (25%)
- **Purpose** : Positionner Clad-Primeco comme expert technique
- **Target** : Specifiers, architects, project managers
- **Formats** : Blog articles, infographics, comparison guides
- **Frequency** : 1x/semaine
- **Examples** : "Kingspan vs Architectural Cladding: Which System?", "Fire Rating Guide for Irish Projects"

### Pillar 3: On-Site Craftsmanship (20%)
- **Purpose** : Montrer le savoir-faire terrain
- **Target** : All audiences
- **Formats** : Reels, Stories, behind-the-scenes, timelapse
- **Frequency** : 2x/semaine
- **Examples** : Panel installation timelapse, tool of the day, weather challenges

### Pillar 4: Irish Construction Insights (15%)
- **Purpose** : Se positionner dans l'écosystème construction irlandais
- **Target** : Industry peers, potential partners
- **Formats** : Blog posts, LinkedIn articles, news commentary
- **Frequency** : 1x/semaine
- **Examples** : TGD Part L compliance guide, Irish construction trends 2026

### Pillar 5: Company Culture & Team (10%)
- **Purpose** : Humaniser la marque, attirer des talents
- **Target** : Potential employees, partners
- **Formats** : Team photos, milestones, safety highlights
- **Frequency** : 1x/semaine
- **Examples** : Meet the team, safety record, Friday site wrap-up

## 6. Content Distribution & Repurposing Plan

### Repurposing Framework (1 Blog = 4+ Pieces)
```
Blog Article (1,500 words)
  → Instagram Carousel (8 slides, key takeaways)
  → LinkedIn Post (300 words, with stat hook)
  → Instagram Reel (30-60s, visual summary)
  → Email Newsletter Section (150 words + link)
  → Twitter/X Thread (5-7 tweets)
```

- Tag suppliers (Kingspan, etc.) in project posts for amplification
- Share in Irish construction LinkedIn groups
- Submit case studies to industry publications (Irish Building Magazine, Construction Magazine)
