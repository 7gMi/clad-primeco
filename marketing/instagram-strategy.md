## 2. Visual Identity Guidelines

### Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Brand Navy (primary) | #1B3564 | Backgrounds, overlays, text frames |
| Cloud White | #F5F7FA | Text on dark backgrounds, borders |
| Steel Grey | #6B7280 | Secondary text, captions |
| Zinc Accent | #B0BEC5 | Metallic shimmer, subtle borders |
| Safety Orange | #E8622A | Accent only — CTAs, highlight badges (sparingly) |

### Photo Style

**Shoot:**
- Wide establishing shots of completed facades at golden hour or overcast daylight
- Close-up texture shots of Kingspan panels, copings, aluminium deck detailing
- Mid-distance shots including workers in PPE for scale
- Before/after split compositions
- Aerial drone shots of large projects (Amazon, Vantage)

**Avoid:**
- Cluttered backgrounds (skips, portable toilets)
- Heavy Instagram filters — edit toward natural tones with slight clarity boost
- Low-resolution or tilted shots
- Faces of workers without consent

### Editing Preset

| Setting | Direction |
|---|---|
| Exposure | +5 to +15 |
| Contrast | +15 to +25 |
| Highlights | -10 to -20 (retain sky detail) |
| Shadows | +10 (panel texture in shade) |
| Color temp | Neutral to slightly cool (5400-5800K) |
| Clarity | +15 to +25 for panel texture posts |
| Saturation | Natural, no oversaturation |

### Grid Layout Pattern (repeat every 6 posts)

```
[Project Photo] [Text Graphic / Stat] [Detail Close-Up]
[BTS Photo]     [Project Photo]       [Team / Culture]
```

### Font Usage

| Role | Font | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Headline | Montserrat Bold / Barlow Condensed Bold | Graphic overlays, stat posts |
| Body | Inter Regular / Nunito Regular | Carousel body copy |
| Accent | Montserrat SemiBold Uppercase | Highlight covers, labels |

**Rules**: Max 2 font weights per graphic. Min 32px for mobile readability. Text on photos must use semi-transparent navy band (#1B3564 at 70-80% opacity).

## 7. Stories & Highlights Structure

### Daily Story Cadence (on posting days)

| Story | Content | Timing |
|---|---|---|
| Story 1 | Feed post teaser (image/3s video clip) | 1-2h before feed post |
| Story 2 | On-site update or quick BTS clip (15s) | Midday |
| Story 3 | Engagement prompt — poll, question box, or slider | Afternoon |
| Story 4 | CTA — "Tap the link" or "Call us for a quote" | 4-5pm |

### Story Templates

1. **Project Teaser** — Navy background, large project photo, white text: "New post — [Project Name]"
2. **Site Update** — Raw on-site video, white text stamp: "On site today — [Location]"
3. **Poll Engagement** — Navy background, question + 2-option poll sticker
4. **Technical Fact Card** — Navy background, bold stat in white Montserrat, body text in Inter
5. **Quote / Testimonial** — Split navy/white, client quote with attribution

### Interactive Features

| Feature | Use | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Poll sticker | Technical opinion polls, material preferences | 2x/week |
| Question box | "Ask us anything about cladding specs" | 1x/week |
| Slider / emoji | "How impressive is this finish?" | 1x/week |
| Link sticker | Drive to quote form, project gallery | Every CTA Story |
| Countdown | Project completions, industry events | As relevant |

### Highlights (max 15 Stories each, audit quarterly)

- **Projects** — 2-3 Stories per completed project (wide shot, close-up, aerial)
- **Systems** — Technical education per material system
- **Process** — BTS installation sequences
- **Team** — Crew intros, milestones, safety, hiring
- **Contact** — Phone, email, map, quote link, "How to work with us"
